Job Title: Data Analyst
Location: Remote
Job Type: Full-time

Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and analytical Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You will be responsible for collecting, processing, and analyzing large datasets to generate actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

Key Responsibilities:

Collect, clean, and analyze structured and unstructured data from various sources.

Build dashboards and reports using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or similar.

Translate complex data into clear business insights and visualizations.

Work with cross-functional teams including marketing, product, and engineering.

Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data to drive business outcomes.

Assist in the development of data pipelines and automated reporting solutions.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.

2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence.

Proficient in SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy), and Excel.

Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Looker).

Strong communication and problem-solving skills.

Familiarity with cloud data platforms (e.g., AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery) is a plus.

Preferred:

Experience working in a fast-paced tech environment.

Knowledge of A/B testing and statistical analysis techniques.
