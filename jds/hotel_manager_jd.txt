Job Title: Hotel General Manager  
Location: Sharm El-Sheikh, Egypt  
Type: Full-time  

We are seeking an experienced and customer-focused Hotel General Manager to oversee daily operations of our beachfront resort. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing all aspects of the hotel including front desk, housekeeping, food and beverage services, finance, and guest relations.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage hotel operations to maximize profitability and guest satisfaction.
- Supervise department heads and ensure consistent service delivery across all units.
- Develop budgets, control costs, and drive revenue growth through marketing initiatives.
- Ensure compliance with local laws, safety regulations, and health standards.
- Manage hiring, training, and performance of staff.
- Resolve guest complaints and implement feedback-driven improvements.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management, Business Administration, or related field.
- 7+ years of experience in hotel management or hospitality operations.
- Proven leadership, financial, and team management skills.
- Fluent in English and Arabic; additional languages are a plus.
- Strong interpersonal and problem-solving abilities.
- Familiarity with hotel management software (e.g., Opera, Protel, Cloudbeds).

Preferred:
- Experience in 4 or 5-star international resort chains.
- Certifications in hospitality or tourism management.
